Welcome

The 2025 Project Management Case Challenge has successfully concluded with 230 participants representing 10 universities and multiple organizations across the region. A comprehensive report detailing the framework, execution model, outcomes, and lessons learned is available here.

Overview

The Project Management Case Challenge is a simulated learning experience designed to provide participants with hands-on experience working through a complete project lifecycle — from initiation to closure — guided by PMI best practices and methodologies.

While each scenario includes guidance in the form of templates and resources, the challenge is designed to encourage independent problem-solving. Participants are expected to conduct their own research, apply critical thinking, and leverage tools such as PMI Infinity to develop and deliver project outputs, mirroring the realities of professional project work.

At the end of the challenge, participants delivered a final presentation showcasing their project management journey — a valuable artifact for any professional portfolio.

This challenge was conceptualized and developed by Yang Ong, in partnership with PMI-LA and UCLA's Master of Applied Statistics & Data Science Program.

Learning Objectives

  • Apply PMI best practices throughout the project lifecycle
  • Develop practical project management skills through hands-on experience
  • Enhance analytical and decision-making capabilities
  • Learn to handle project changes and risks effectively
  • Create professional project deliverables and presentations

Grand Prize

The winning team received complimentary tickets (valued at $120 each) to PMI-LA's Professional Development Day on October 25, 2025.

Timeline

Participants had four weeks to complete all modules, with all deliverables submitted by October 6, 2025.
